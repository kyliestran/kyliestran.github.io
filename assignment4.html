Accessibility Statement

One accessibility concern is that users may have a hard time going back and forth between pages. Although they all do link to the right pages, going back to the homepage may be difficult for users to manuver. One way to help users it to maybe have a link at the top of all my pages. Another issue with my page can be the little to no contrast. This may make navigating and reading my page more difficult for those who are visually impared and rely on contrast to help them see. I could potentially add more color and contrasting text.
